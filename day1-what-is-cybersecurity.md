# Day 1 - What is Cybersecurity?

## CIA Triad
- Confidentiality: only authorised users are allowed to access data 
- Integrity: data must not be tampered with
- Availability: system must be accessible when needed

## Real World Examples
- Confidentiality example: your medical records being private
- Integrity example: a bank transfer amount not being altered
- Availability example:  a hospital system going down during an emergency

## Types of Attackers
- Black Hat: malicious hacker, attacks for personal gain
- White Hat: ethical hacker, hired to find vulnerabilities
- Grey Hat: in between, hacks without permission but no bad intent

## What I Learned Today  
The true meaning of cybersecurity is the CIA Triad because it captures everything cybersecurity stands for — protecting data, keeping it accurate, and ensuring it's always available.
